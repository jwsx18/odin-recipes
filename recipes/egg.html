Egg in a hole

Description

Egg in a hole makes a delicious breakfast! A runny yolk egg cooked in the center of a piece of toast and eaten with a fork is a great way to enjoy and egg in a slightly different way.

Ingredients:

  • Bacon grease: 1.5 tablespoon of bacon grease for cooking
  • Bread: A slice of bread as a base.
  • Salt and Ground black pepper: These will add taste.
  • Egg: The main ingredient.

Steps:

  1. Melt bacon grease in a nonstick pan over low heat.
  2. Cut a hole in the center of bread slice and place it in the hot skillet for 2 minutes.
  3. Flip the bread and crack an egg into the hole.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Continue cooking until the egg is cooked and mostly firm.
  6. Flip again and cook for a single minute.
  7. Serve the meal.
