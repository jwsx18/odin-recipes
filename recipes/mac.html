Mac and Cheese

Description

This mac and cheese recipe with a buttered bread cumb topping is creamy and comforting. It is easy to make the cheese sauce from scratch on your stovetop. You can serve this comfort-food macaroni casserole with grilled meats, fried chickens or alongside a salad.

Ingredients:

  • Macaroni: This homemade mac and cheese starts with a box of uncooked macaroni noodles.
  • Milk: Use whole milk for the richest flavor and texture.
  • Cheese: Preferably cheddar or permesan.
  • Seasonings: Salt and pepper into the sauce, paprika sprinkled over the topping.
  • Butter and Flour: These are used to make a roux for the cheese sauce. You will also need two tablespoons of butter for the topping.
  • Bread crumbs: Use store-bought dried bread crumbs.

Steps:

  1. Boil the macaroni in salted water until noodles are ready. Drain and transfer to prepared baking dish.
  2. Melt butter, then whisk the flour. Whisk in milk, bring to a simmer, and stir in the cheese.
  3. Season with pepper and salt, continue simmering until the sauce is thick.
  4. Pour the sauce over the noodles and stir.
  5. Melt two tablespoons of butter in a skilled, add bread crumbs, and toast until the crumbs are brown.
  6. Spread the topping over the macaroni and cheese, then sprinkle with paprika.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until the topping is golden brown.
  8. Serve the dish.
